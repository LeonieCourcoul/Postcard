Lonie Courcoul

Bio

Hi ! Im Lonie Courcoul. I am a PhD student in Biostatistics at the University of Bordeaux (France), in the Biostatistic team from Bordeaux Population Health Center (INSERM U1219) since November 2021. I do my research under the supervision of Hlne Jacqmin-Gadda and Antoine Barbieri. My thesis work are focused on the development of statistical models to study the impact of the variability of blood pressure on the risk of various clinical events such as stroke and dementia.
